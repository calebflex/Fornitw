Top Competitive Tips & Mistakes to Avoid

  • Don’t hot-drop busy POIs like Supernova Academy without a plan
  • Always plan your rotation to avoid getting caught in storm
  • Use Boons & Hero Ranks to your advantage
  • Keep your crosshair at head level for faster kills
  • After kills, create space to avoid 3rd parties
  • Maintain material count, don’t waste them early
  • Don’t overextend without vision or height control
  • Master piece control and right-hand peek edits
  • Use mobility items smartly, don’t waste them
  • Balance eliminations with placement points

Best Drop Spots (Chapter 6 Season 3)

  • Fortress of Solitude: Safe, great loot, slurp barrels & vehicles
  • Craggy Cliffs: Elevation advantage, fishing spots, safe rotations
  • Salty Docks: Boats for rotation, many chests, less contest
  • Dirty Docks: High chest spawn rate, container loot, NPC upgrades
  • Mountain Tunnel: Low contest, great vehicle spawns
  • Ranger's Ruin: Hero shrine, mythic chance, good materials
  • Supernova Academy: Mythics & vaults but very high risk

Rotation Strategy

  • Land on edge POIs to minimize early fights
  • Loot quickly (3-4 min max) and secure shields
  • Rotate early towards shrines or spires for Boons
  • Use vehicles, launch pads, and natural cover
  • Save mobility items for late game escapes or fights
  • Predict storm movement & rotate to high ground

Tournament Step-by-Step Plan

  1. Before game: Warm-up aim & movement (15-20 min)
  2. Landing: Choose safe or contested POIs based on lobby
  3. Early game: Focus on looting & avoiding fights
  4. Mid game: Prioritize rotations & upgrade Boons
  5. Late game: Control high ground & conserve mats
  6. End game: Use smart aggression & third-party fights
  7. After match: Review replays and mistakes
